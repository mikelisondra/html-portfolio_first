The best Movies According to Mike

My top 10 movies of all-time

Forrest Gump

This is my favorite movie of all.

The Wind Rises

This is my favorite anime. Its inspiring mood makes me view my goals seriously.

Schindler's List

This movie shows the other side of humanity, that behind every evil there is still a human weeping.

Parasite

This is one of the most amazing movies I have ever watched!

Godzilla Minus One

This movie is the newest one on my favorites. The story is good, the characters are great, and the CGI's are amazing.

The Greatest Showman

This movie is really inspiring!

The White Tiger

This movie shows how dark reality can be, how low people can go to, and how high one's ambition can be given the correct situation. I like it.

12th Fail

This is also a new movie, released on 2023. I recently failed one of my subject myself and I find this movie kind of relatable in a sense. I find the characters and the story really inspiring.

Napoleon

This movie is great. Who doesnt like conquering? who doesnt like Napoleon? even the British likes him.

3 Idiots

This movie is why I am still around doing my things. Like the characters in this movie, whenever something I like doesnt come around the way I hope it would, I always take it as a challenge and still do my best. To hope that "All is well".

These are my favorite movies for now.
Like life, my favorite movies are not constant.
